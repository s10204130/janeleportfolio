Information


This project required me to create a 10s production trailer. I chose to make the trailer about my running club, AGT/Trackstar. The main aim of the trailer was to encourage potential runners seeking professional coaching to join the team.
I had to ideate on the scene breakdowns, the location and time, the actors and the overall mood of the trailer.
This was a personal project. Hence, all scenes and editing was done by me.

Tools Used In Project


Adobe Premier Pro, Adobe Photoshop (Team Logo), Pixaby (BGM)
